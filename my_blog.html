  • The Commute to Google

    • The commute to google was something I was very nervous about. I never have been on public transportation, especially not alone. I had to travel from Garfield, Nj to New York City. Thaat is something that made me extremely anxious considering I have always been prone to being clumsy and getting lost. The first day I was so anxious and was trying to find my way around everything from train to train. I even accidentally activated the wrong tickey for my train and had to buy a new one. However, I feel in time I may be able to get used to this commute. It is something that I am learning from and an experience that I had to encounter in my life. It is teaching me how to become more independent and feel more comfortable.
